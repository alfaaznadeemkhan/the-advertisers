Common Questions

Answers to help you get started.

How long does an audio ad take to produce?

Typical turnaround for a 30-60 second audio ad is 24-48 hours after script approval and payment. Complex jingles or multi-voice ads may take slightly longer.

Can you help me write the script?

Yes! We offer scriptwriting and editing services. We can help you craft a message that is clear, concise, and effective for your target audience. Just let us know you need this service during our consultation.

What file formats will I receive?

We provide files in any format you need. The most common are high-quality MP3 (for web and social media) and uncompressed WAV (for broadcast). For video, we typically deliver MP4 files.

How does the payment process work?

We require full payment upfront for most standard projects after the initial consultation and quote approval. For large-scale web development projects, we typically arrange a 50% deposit to start and 50% upon completion.
