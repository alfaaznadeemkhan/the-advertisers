Our Story

1985 - The Beginning

Founded in Barabanki, "The Advertisers" opens its doors, focusing on high-quality audio production for local businesses and public announcements.

2000s - Building a Legacy

Became the trusted voice for major campaigns, from "Chunao Prachar" to large-scale school and hospital advertisements, building a 40+ year legacy of trust.

2020s - The Next Generation

The next generation joins, bringing software engineering expertise. We expand our services to include video ads and modern web development (Spring Boot, Python, AI).

Today - Nationwide Reach

We now serve clients all over India and the world through our seamless remote production process, blending our rich history with cutting-edge technology.
